About Us

Our Industry

The tech industry is responsible for about 2 to 3% of the world's carbon emissions, the same percentage as the emissions released by aviation. This impact will only increase as growth sectors like AI, online gaming and streaming services need more computing power.

Our Mission

SUMA helps tech startups, growth companies and ecosystems like VC funds and blockchains to become future-proof. By adopting sustainability practices and working towards zero emissions and carbon neutrality.

Our Team

SUMA SAS is based in France, a leading country for the movement of climate action. Our company is founded by a team with extensive experience in tech platforms, internet services and blockchain.

We believe that Europe is setting the standard and has some exciting up and coming things in the cryptocurrency area, like The Markets in Crypto Assets (MiCA) regulation which will go live within the next two years. SUMA will assist with the understanding and the implementing of the EU rules for sustainability and ESG, helping your business adapt to the future.
