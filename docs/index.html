Client Demand

Clients are demanding sustainability. They want to know that the companies they buy from are committed to protecting the environment and making a positive social impact. Sustainability reporting shows to your customers that you are taking action to do business, while calculating your impact.

Investor Focus

Sustainability reporting shows your investors that you are a well-managed company with a strong commitment to ESG (Environmental, Social and Governance).

Partner Requirements

Leading companies are increasingly looking to partner with companies that share their commitment to sustainability.

Platform Features

Carbon Accounting

Based on the popular GHG Protocol and designed particularly for tech companies, our carbon accounting tool enables you to calculate accurate carbon footprint, including energy consumption of your cloud services, blockchain holdings and transactions.

Decarbonisation Assistance

Once you know your carbon footprint, the next step is to work on short and long term goals towards carbon neutrality. Our unique AI-agent works as a virtual Sustainability Manager, advising and assisting you on carbon footprint reduction, specific to your company’s needs.

Carbon Offsetting

Companies who make use of carbon offsetting, reach their sustainability goals faster. On SUMA, you can offset your carbon footprint with high-quality, top-rated carbon credits supplied by our verified partner.

Sustainability & ESG Reporting

SUMA Reports is our unique tool where you can easily create reports and publish them on your website. Because you want to show your company’s efforts and be proud to publish it. Adding a “Sustainability” section to your website is a great way to keep your clients up to date about your sustainability journey. You can also use these reports as a part of your standard financial reporting.  

Blockchain Carbon Footprint Calculator

The crypto sector has often been criticised for its significant carbon footprint, due to the computing power required by decentralised networks, also known as blockchains

SUMA Blockchain Carbon Calculator enables crypto industry participants, such as traders, funds, and DeFi platforms, to calculate their carbon footprint based on their crypto holdings, transactions and other on-chain activity.

Our accounting method is based on the GHG Protocol and the Crypto Carbon Rating Institute’s methodology.

Carbon Calculator currently supports Bitcoin, Ethereum, Polygon, and BNB Chain, and is expanding to support all top blockchain networks, cryptocurrencies, tokens, and stablecoins. Soon, the calculator will also provide ESG ratings and MiCA-compliant reporting, which is coming in 2024.
