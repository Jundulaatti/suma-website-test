Why ESG
Matters

Client Demand

Clients are demanding sustainability. They want to know that the companies they buy from are committed to protecting the environment and making a positive social impact. Sustainability reporting helps you show customers that you are serious about sustainability and that you are taking action to make a difference.

Investor Focus

Investors are increasingly focused on ESG. Environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors are now a major consideration for investors when making investment decisions. Sustainability reporting helps you show investors that you are a well-managed company with a strong commitment to ESG.

Partner Requirements

Businesses are looking for sustainable partners. Leading companies are increasingly looking to partner with other companies that share their commitment to sustainability. Sustainability reporting helps you show potential partners that you are a sustainable company that they can trust.

Platform Features

Carbon Accounting

Based on the popular GHG Protocol and designed particularly for tech companies, our carbon accounting tool enables you to calculate accurate carbon footprint including energy consumption of your cloud services and blockchain holdings and transactions.  

Decarbonisation Assistance

Once you know your carbon footprint, the next step is to working on consistently on short and long term goals towards carbon neutrality. Our unique AI-agent works like a virtual Sustainaiblity Manager advising and assist you on carbon footprint reduction.  

Carbon Offsetting

Research shows that company who also do carbon offsetting, reach their sustainability goals faster than those who don’t. On SUMA you can offset your carbon footprint with high-quality, third-party top-rated carbon credits. 

Sustainability & ESG Reporting

EU is a global leader on sustainability regulations and directives. With SUMA Reports tool you can easily create Sustainability and ESG reports and publish them publicly on your website and as a part of your standard financial reporting. Reports are great addion to your marketing as well. 

Blockchain Carbon Footprint Calculator

The crypto sector has often been criticized for its significant carbon footprint, due to the computing power required by decentralized networks, also known as blockchains

SUMA Blockchain Carbon Calculator enables crypto industry participants, such as traders, funds, and DeFi platforms, to calculate their carbon footprint based on their crypto holdings, transactions and other on-chain activity.

Our accounting method is based on the GHG Protocol and the Crypto Carbon Rating Institute’s methodology.

Carbon Calculator currently supports Bitcoin, Ethereum, Polygon, and BNB Chain, and is expanding to support all top blockchain networks, cryptocurrencies, tokens, and stablecoins. Soon, the calculator will also provide ESG ratings and MiCA-compliant reporting.
